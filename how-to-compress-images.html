How to Compress Images Without Losing Quality

Image compression is essential for improving website performance, but it’s important to maintain quality. Here are some tips:

  • Use the right file format (JPEG for photos, PNG for graphics).
  • Adjust the compression level to balance size and quality.
  • Use tools like our Image Compressor to simplify the process.

By following these steps, you can ensure your images load quickly without looking pixelated or blurry.
